# Day-3 : Designing Library Cells using Magic and ngspice Tools

Day 3 focused on the design and characterization of a custom **CMOS inverter standard cell** using **Magic** and **ngspice**. The objective was to understand the complete standard cell design flow, starting from layout creation and verification, followed by SPICE extraction and simulation. The custom inverter was then characterized and prepared for integration into the OpenLANE physical design flow in the subsequent stages.
